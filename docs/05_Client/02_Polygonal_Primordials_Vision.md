# 02_Polygonal_Primordials_Vision

This document specifies the artistic theme, narrative setting, and visual identity of Polygonal Primordials.

---

## 1. Narrative Setting
* **The Geometry Space:** The simulation takes place in a flat, infinite grid universe where physical matter and intelligence are expressed exclusively through polygons.
* **The Evolution of Order:** Simple triangles and squares evolve from mindless resource gatherers into organized societies, discovering that specialization increases survival efficiency.

---

## 2. Visual Style Guidelines
* **Vibrant Minimalist Art:** Shapes utilize flat, semi-transparent vector geometries with bright HSL color scales representing emotional states (see [07_Visual_Aesthetics_Guide](file:///C:/Users/ASUS/Documents/Primodilas/docs/05_Client/07_Visual_Aesthetics_Guide.md)).
* **Neon Glow Effects:** High-speed movements, physical collisions, and Sentinel fields project soft neon glow lines, indicating energy dissipation.
* **Information Density:** Replaces complex textures with clean data lines. Pathing vectors, influence fields, and trade highways are rendered directly on the screen, creating a premium interface.
