# 03_Universe_Principles

This document formalizes the universal principles of energy, matter, and information exchange that direct the simulation universe.

---

## 1. Conservation of Mass & Matter
* **Law:** Matter cannot appear from nowhere or vanish.
* **Implementation:** The sum of all active particles and shape masses is constant unless modified by explicit chemical or biological conversions.
* **Recycling:** Deceased entities decay into static food resources:
  $$\text{Mass}_{\text{food}} = \text{Mass}_{\text{deceased}}$$

---

## 2. Thermodynamics of Energy
* **Law:** Energy is converted, stored, and dissipated — never created or destroyed.
* **Metabolism:** Steering forces consume kinetic energy:
  $$\Delta E = -C_{\text{metabolism}} \times |v| \times \Delta t$$
* **Dissipation:** Unused kinetic force dissipates via damping (simulating heat/friction).
* **Energy Limits:** Entities starve if energy reaches 0.0, and split (reproduce) if energy exceeds a threshold.

---

## 3. Propagation of Information
* **Law:** Information behaves like a physical quantity with entropy and bandwidth limits.
* **Transmission:** Information spreads via sensory perception, field emissions, and shared Sentinel memory.
* **Entropy & Decay:** Memories grow old and decay in strength unless reinforced:
  $$\text{Strength}_{t+1} = \text{Strength}_t - R_{\text{decay}} \times \Delta t \quad (\text{if age} > T_{\text{threshold}})$$
